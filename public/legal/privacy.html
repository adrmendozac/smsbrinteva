Privacy Policy

Brinteva Worlds Inc. · Effective date: June 28, 2026

Brinteva Worlds Inc. ("Brinteva Worlds," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have, including with respect to text (SMS) messages.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, mobile phone number, and details related to travel bookings and purchases. We also collect basic technical information when you interact with our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our travel services, process bookings, respond to your inquiries, and — where you have agreed — to send you promotional offers, booking and account notifications, and customer-care messages by SMS and other channels.

SMS / Text Messaging

If you provide your mobile number and consent to receive text messages, we may send you recurring marketing, transactional, and customer-care SMS. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, or get help by replying HELP. See our SMS Terms & Conditions for full details.

We do not sell your information. No mobile information (including phone numbers and SMS opt-in/consent data) will be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third party.

How We Share Information

We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business (for example, payment processing, messaging delivery, and IT services), strictly to perform services on our behalf and subject to confidentiality obligations. We may also disclose information when required by law. As stated above, we do not share SMS opt-in or consent data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide our services and to comply with our legal obligations. You may request deletion of your information by contacting us.

Your Choices

You may opt out of marketing communications at any time. For SMS, reply STOP to unsubscribe. You may also contact us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you.
