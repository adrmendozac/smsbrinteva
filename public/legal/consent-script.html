SMS Verbal Consent Script

Brinteva Worlds Inc. · Live-operator (phone / WhatsApp) opt-in · Effective: June 28, 2026

This is the script Brinteva Worlds Inc. agents use to obtain a customer's verbal consent to receive text messages while booking a travel package by phone or WhatsApp. Agents must read the consent request and record the customer's affirmative answer before any SMS is sent.

1. After collecting the booking details and mobile number

English

"Is the number you gave me a mobile number that can receive text messages? May we send you booking updates and occasional promotional travel offers by text to this number? You'll receive recurring messages, message and data rates may apply, and message frequency varies. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for help. Do I have your permission?"

Español

"¿El número que me dio es un número celular que puede recibir mensajes de texto? ¿Nos permite enviarle actualizaciones de su reserva y ofertas promocionales de viaje por mensaje de texto a este número? Recibirá mensajes recurrentes, pueden aplicar tarifas de mensajes y datos, y la frecuencia varía. Puede responder STOP en cualquier momento para cancelar, o HELP para ayuda. ¿Me da su permiso?"

2. Record consent

The agent records the customer's affirmative "yes" (with date and the agent's name) before the number is added to the messaging list. If the customer declines, the number is not enrolled. Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

3. Confirmation

Customers who consent may receive a confirmation message and can opt out at any time by replying STOP, or get help by replying HELP. Full details are in our SMS Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.
